About

Paul Olayemi Binoran

Olayemi is an ordained Pastor of the Redeemed Christian Church of God with a unique flare of teaching God's Word.
He comes from a great heritage of royalty in Odulu, Kabba in Kogi.

Olayemi has been a writer all of his adult life. A prolific writer, he has MSc in Public Administration, and MA in Christian Leadership.
Trained as a Soil & Water Engineer, Production and Operations Manager, Public Administrator.
His major objective is his teaching and preaching is to recover men to the true reality of God's Word.
He is a versatile reader, and a prolific author, an ardent worshipper of God in songs, known for his adept interpretation of Scriptures and fervent prayers as encapsulated in Acts 6:4.

Olayemi embodies and emphasises key spiritual truths rallying unique blend of different graces, gifts and talents, thus prepares the church for a deeper experience of God's presence and other vital scripture subjects.
He uses ministry oppurtunity to impart, and serves as a preacher who advocates for the right interpretation of scriptures.
Engulfed with a passion and utmost dynamism to spread the message of the pursuit of God following the excercise of unique grace.
He ministers with a strong grace and annointing, leading the Body of Christ into great experience of the glory of God.
He is married to Pastor Violet Olayemi for about two decades, and are blessed with children.
