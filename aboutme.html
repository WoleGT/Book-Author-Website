ABOUT

Binoran Paul Olayemi Martins

Olayemi is an ordained Pastor of the Redeemed Christian Church Of God with a unique flare of teaching God's Word.
He comes from a great heritage of royalty in Odolu, Kabba, in Kogi State. He has been a writer all of his adult life and has MSc in Public Administration and MA in Christian Leadership.

His major objective in his teaching and preaching is to recover men to the true reality of God's Word. He is an ardent worshipper of God in songs, a versatile reader and a prolific author known for adept interpretation of Scriptures and fervent prayers as encapsulated in Act 6:4. Olayemi embodies and emphasises key spiritual truths rallying unique blend of different graces, gifts and talents.
He uses ministry opportunities to impart and serve as a preacher who advocates for right interpretation of the Bible. He is engulfed with a passion and utmost dynamism to spread the message of the pursuit of God imbued with the exercise of unique grace. He ministers in grace and anointing culminating into great experiences to the glory of God.

He is married to Pastor Violet Ediri Olayemi for about two decades, and are blessed with children.
